Design rules:
Use serif headings like Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or similar.
Use clean sans-serif body text like Inter, Manrope, or similar.
Colors:
- Deep black / charcoal
- Warm ivory
- Antique gold
- Muted grey text
Cards should feel editorial and premium, not SaaS bubbly.
Avoid bright gradients, cartoon icons, purple/blue tech styling, fake statistics, and generic startup language.
Use subtle scroll fades, gold hover states, soft shadows, thin dividers, and generous spacing.

The final page should feel like a luxury archive, a legal-tech product, and a creative protection movement all in one.
